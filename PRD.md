# PRD.md: Product Strategy

## Vision
To create an immersive, cinematic digital resume that captures the dual professional identity of Frank Hanson: a rigorous Data Analytics/Operations leader and a creative Cinematographer.

## User Intent Mapping
- **Critical / Transactional (Recruiters/Hiring Managers)**:
    - Quick access to Experience, Education, and Skills.
    - Ability to download/view traditional PDF resumes.
    - Clear contact information.
- **Exploratory / Social (Portfolio Viewers/Creative Leads)**:
    - High-quality visual portfolio showcase.
    - Drone cinematography and event photography samples.
    - Interactive "Netflix" experience.

## Feature Heuristics
| Feature | User Intent | Complexity | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Experience Rows** | Critical | Medium | Must provide quick scanning of professional history with modal deep-dives. |
| **Portfolio Showcase** | Exploratory | High | Focus on visual excellence and cinematic presentation. |
| **Audio Jingle** | Social | Low | Enhances brand identity without blocking information flow. |
| **Contact Modal** | Transactional | Low | Minimal friction for professional outreach. |

## Success Metrics
- **Engagement**: Time spent on page and number of modals opened.
- **Retention**: Return visits from tracked LinkedIn links.
- **Conversion**: Inbound contact requests and LinkedIn profile views.
